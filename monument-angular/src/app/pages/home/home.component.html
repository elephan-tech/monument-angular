More than just a school, a home.

Our Mission

To provide students, particularly those who have had or might have contact with the foster care system, with the requisite academic, social, emotional, and life skills to be successful in college, career, and community, and to create an outstanding school that attracts, supports, and retains exceptional and caring people.

We Change Lives

We believe all students have the capacity to succeed at high academic levels and experience positive life outcomes.

Family-Style Boarding

Students board at Monument Academy from Sunday evening through to Friday afternoon. They live in small, family-style single gender homes, with two houseparents for each home of 10 students.

Students receive a personal wake-up and eat a family-style breakfast every morning with their Houseparent and Student Life Family. Students share a personal daily goal with their family and head to advisory for community building.
