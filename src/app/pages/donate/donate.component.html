Monument Academy’s mission is to provide vulnerable students – marked by trauma and family instability, particularly those who have had or might have contact with the foster care system – with the requisite academic, social, emotional, and life skills to be successful in college, career, and community, and to create an outstanding school that attracts, supports, and retains exceptional and caring staff.
