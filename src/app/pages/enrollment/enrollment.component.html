Join us at Monument Academy! We are enrolling 5th, 6th, 7th, and 8th grade students who are residents of Washington, D.C. Read on to learn more.

Who can apply?

We are serving 5th, 6th, 7th, and 8th-grade students in the 2026-27 school year.

How do I apply?

Monument Academy participates in the My School DC lottery. Create an account and apply at MySchoolDC. You can reach our Student Information Manager, Lakisha Lyles at lakisha.lyles@mapcsdc.org. You can also call the school at (202) 545-3180

What if the lottery date has already passed?

Monument Academy will continue to accept applications after the lottery date. Once all spaces are filled, applicants will be added to the waiting list. Please click on the link below to reach the My School DC lottery site, and click on the “Submit your post-lottery application today” button to apply.

Apply Now!

What documents do I need?

The following documents are required in order to hold your spot at Monument Academy for the 2026-27 school year:

  • Completed student enrollment form.

  • Proof of DC residency and a signed DC Residency Verification Form (Bring all documents in-person, directly to the campus. Documents must be originals, not copies, with the name of the parent or legal guardian enrolling the student)

      • Acceptable Proof of DC Residency

      We recommend ONE of the following:

    • A pay stub, issued within 45 days with your DC address and DC taxes. Maryland and Virginia taxes being withheld are not valid.

    • Proof of financial assistance from the DC Government on official letterhead or sent directly from a DC office, such as Housing Assistance, TANF, or Food Stamps, issued within the past 12 months.

    • Supplemental Security Income (SSI) annual benefits notification

    • Verification Letter and Military Housing Orders

      • Or TWO of the following with the same name and address on both documents:

    • Unexpired DC motor vehicle registration

    • Unexpired lease or rental agreement

    • Unexpired DC motor vehicle operator’s permit (or other official non-driver identification)

    • Utility bill (ONLY gas, electric, and water are acceptable), issued within the past two months.

  • DC Universal Health Certificate (form available here)

    • For any children who have health forms expiring before September 1, 2026, new Health Certificates must be submitted.

    • Please note that all children must be fully immunized according to the DC Health School Immunization Requirements Guide

  • DC Oral Health Assessment Form (form available here)

    • For any children who have oral health forms expiring before September 1, 2026, new forms must be submitted.

  • Student’s Birth Certificate

  • Parent/Guardian Proof of Identity

    • Must be submitted in person and be an unexpired legal form of identification (e.g., driver's license, state ID, or passport).

  • Entire Special Education File (if applicable)

  • Final (4th Quarter) Report Card from Current School (once available)

IMPORTANT NOTICE: If all required documents are not submitted, your student may be moved to the waitlist if the school reaches full enrollment.
