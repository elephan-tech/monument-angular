Join us at Monument Academy! We are enrolling 5th, 6th, 7th, and 8th grade students who are residents of Washington, D.C. Read on to learn more.

Who can apply?

We are serving 5th, 6th, 7th, and 8th grade students in the 2021-22 year.

How do I apply?

Monument Academy participates in the My School DC lottery. You can reach our Registrar and Engagement Coordinator, Lakisha Lyles at lakisha.lyles@mapcsdc.org. You can also call the school at (202) 545-3180 to set up an appointment to speak about applying and enrolling. Please click on the link below to reach the My School DC lottery site.

What if the lottery date has already passed?

Monument Academy will continue to accept applications after the lottery date. Once all spaces are filled, applicants will be added to the waiting list. Please click on the link below to reach the My School DC lottery site, and click on the “Submit your post-lottery application today” button to apply.

What documents do I need?

The following documents are required in order to hold your spot at Monument Academy for the 2021-22 school year:

  • Completed student enrollment form (you will fill this out when you meet with the Monument Academy team)

  • My School DC Seat Acceptance Form (form available here)

  • Proof of DC residency and a signed DC Residency Verification Form (Bring all documents in-person, directly to the campus. Documents must be originals, not copies, with the name of the parent or legal guardian enrolling the student)

      • We recommend ONE of the following:

    • A pay stub, issued within 45 days with your DC address and DC taxes. Maryland and Virginia pay stubs are not valid.

    • Proof of financial assistance from the DC Government on official letterhead or sent directly from a DC office, such as Housing Assistance, TANF, or Food Stamps.

    • Supplemental Security Income (SSI) annual benefits notification

    • Verification Letter and Military Housing Orders

      • Or TWO of the following with the same name and address on both documents:

    • Unexpired DC motor vehicle registration

    • Unexpired lease or rental agreement

    • Unexpired DC motor vehicle operator’s permit (or other official non-driver identification)

    • Utility bill (ONLY gas, electric, and water are acceptable)

  • DC Universal Health Certificate (form available here)

    • For any children who have health forms expiring before September 1, 2021, new Health Certificates must be submitted.

  • DC Oral Health Assessment Form (form available here)

    • For any children who have oral health forms expiring before September 1, 2021, new forms must be submitted.

  • Home Language Survey: access here

  • Student’s Birth Certificate

  • Parent/Guardian Proof of Identity

    • Please submit in person. Can be an unexpired legal form of identification like a Drivers License, State ID, or Passport.

  • Entire Special Education File (if applicable)

  • Final (4th Quarter) Report Card from Current School (once available)

  • If you do not provide these items, you may be moved to the wait list in the event that we are fully enrolled.
