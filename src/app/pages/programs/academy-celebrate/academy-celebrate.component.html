Monument Academy Celebrates Excellence!

In December, Monument Academy celebrated all that we accomplished in 2023 and announced our Teacher of the Year and Staff Member of the Year at our 2nd annual Monumental Achievement Gala! These awards are given to individuals who exemplify what it means to be present and engaged and go above and beyond for our school and students. We are excited to announce our winners, Pushaen Gunasinghe and Shoshanna Gleaton, and are so grateful for their contributions to the Monument Academy community! Congratulations!

After awards were presented, a celebration ensued at the Watergate among Monument Academy staff, board members, supporters, community partners, family, and friends!
